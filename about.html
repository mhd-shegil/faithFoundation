Who We are?

FAITH Foundation is the result of a collaborative effort 10 students who wanted to create an impact in society by investing their ideas and skills. We were part of scouts, guides, NSS and been part of various social initiatives during their school and College days, and they brought along the values they learned from those schemes to create a like minded group even though they are currently studying in different central and state universities across India. We made Youth as the prime target group of intervention as they are going to lead the next generation which requires various skills and capabilities.

Faith Foundation Overview

Faith Foundation is a youth-led initiative committed to driving positive change in society. Established by a group of passionate students united by a shared vision, the foundation focuses on addressing social challenges through innovative solutions and community-driven programs.We believe in empowering young minds to take an active role in shaping their communities. With a strong emphasis on social responsibility, our mission is to create a platform where youth can collaborate, lead, and contribute towards building a more inclusive and equitable society.From grassroots initiatives to strategic collaborations, Faith Foundation is dedicated to making a tangible impact and inspiring others to join the journey of social transformation.
