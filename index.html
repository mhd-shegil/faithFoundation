Who We Are

This is a collaborative effort of 10 students who wanted to create an impact in society by investing their ideas and skills. Our emphasis lies in creating a platform where youth can collaborate, lead, and contribute towards building a more inclusive and equitable society.

Our Objectives: Guiding Principles for Meaningful Impact

  • To empower youth to take active roles in community development.
  • To create a platform for meaningful youth engagement and leadership.
  • To nurture a culture of social responsibility and collaboration.
  • To enhance life skills and promote holistic well-being among youth.
  • To build a resilient and informed community through education and training.

Our Vision and Mission: Guiding Lights for a Better Tomorrow.

We are driven by a clear vision to empower youth and foster thriving communities. Our mission focuses on education, health, and social development to create meaningful and lasting change.

Our Vision

To build a community where youth lead the way in creating meaningful change, ensuring safety, growth, and sustainability for future generations.

Our Mission

To empower young individuals by providing them with essential skills, leadership opportunities, and a platform to drive impactful change within their communities.

BLS Training for Schools under Kottakkal Municipality

This BLS Training program, offered to schools under Kottakkal Municipality, aims to teach students essential life-saving skills. Participants will learn CPR, choking management, and basic life support techniques to be prepared for emergencies and help save lives.

28 Jan, 2025

BLS Training for Volunteers: Equipping You to Save Lives

Our Volunteer BLS Training equips participants with the essential skills to respond effectively in life-threatening situations. Learn how to perform CPR, manage choking, and provide basic life support to save lives in emergencies.
