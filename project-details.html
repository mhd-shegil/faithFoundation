BLS Training for Schools under Kottakkal Municipality

This BLS Training program, offered to schools under Kottakkal Municipality, aims to teach students essential life-saving skills. Participants will learn CPR, choking management, and basic life support techniques to be prepared for emergencies and help save lives.

As part of our mission to build a safer and more prepared society, the BLS Training Program is being extended to schools, public institutions, and community groups within the Kottakkal Municipality. This initiative is designed to empower individuals with essential life-saving skills and practical emergency response knowledge.


What Participants Will Learn

  • Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
  • Choking management techniques
  • Basic Life Support (BLS) fundamentals
  • Emergency scene assessment and response
  • Airway clearance and rescue breathing

Conducted in collaboration with certified trainers, this hands-on program is designed to ensure that every participant is equipped to act quickly and confidently in emergency situations. By fostering awareness and building critical skills, the training helps develop a community of proactive and responsible first responders.

Location: Various centers under Kottakkal Municipality

Objective: To create a prepared, responsive, and health-aware community
